Notices

By-Elections

Anyone who wants to contest by-elections on behalf of Federal Party SA (FPSA) should contact FPSA, to make arrangements. The list of by-elections gives dates, municipalities and wards where by-elections will be held.

New Name

Divided Party (DiP) changed its name to Federal Party SA (FPSA), because the meaning of divide, in English, does not have the same meaning, "verdeel" (divide) to "deel" (share) in Afrikaans. Notification about acceptence of the new name, from the Independent Electoral Commission (IEC) was received on 27 August 2020.
