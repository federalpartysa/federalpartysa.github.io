Municipalities

Strategy

FPSA is registered nationally. Therefore, anyone can represent his/her group in any municipality or province, or nationally, whilst earning non-party funded salaries.

Doing away or reducing the size of provincial governments and municipalities, is a possibility; having basically just two levels of government; national and districts. National government should be the smallest possible. District map of South Africa. Members of the national government can focus on districts they represent.
